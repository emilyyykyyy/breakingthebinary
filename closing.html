break the binary

blackout poems to break the binary in bucks county


bucks school districts has long perpetuated the constant transphobia and homophobia occurring in bucks schools. through book bans, disregarding gender nonconforming students' identities, and prohibiting pride flags - schools have become an unsafe place for trans students.

schools should be a welcoming place for everyone, but it couldn't be anything further than that. district administration and board have turned a blind eye to discrimination; moreover, they have actively sought out policies that target genderqueer individuals, furthering a harmful environment for learning.

trans students need safe spaces to learn. through hateful and discriminatory policies, bucks county schools have only furthered gender inequity and injustice.

- signed, a central bucks student (e.k.)
